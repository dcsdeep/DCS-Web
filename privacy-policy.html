Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 6, 2025

At Digital Crew Solution ("we", "our", or "us"), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit or use our website https://digitalcrewsolution.com (the "Service").

By accessing or using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Personal Information

We may collect personally identifiable information, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Mailing address (including city, state, postal code)
  • Any other information you choose to provide

Usage Data

We automatically collect data about your interaction with our site, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Pages visited
  • Time and date of visit
  • Time spent on pages
  • Referral source

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies (like beacons and scripts) to:

  • Store preferences
  • Understand usage patterns
  • Improve user experience
  • Support security features

You can manage cookie settings through your browser. Some cookies are essential for site functionality.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information for purposes such as:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Responding to inquiries and support requests
  • Managing user accounts
  • Sending marketing communications (with consent)
  • Improving website performance and security
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Analyzing website usage trends and engagement

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for:

Your data may be stored or processed outside your country of residence. We take appropriate measures to ensure data protection regardless of location, in compliance with applicable laws.
